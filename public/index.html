    AI-driven CRM workflow for WhatsApp and E-mail

    Aug 2025 - Nov 2025

    At Inorbvict Healthcare, a medical equipment and devices provider, the goal was to modernize how inbound leads are handled across WhatsApp, email and the internal CRM so that every inquiry passes through a consistent, intelligent sales funnel.

    Business Value Delivered
    • Annual cost savings: $13,500 in reduced manual labor
    • Revenue impact: 28% increase in qualified lead conversion
    • Scalability: Handles 500% more leads without additional staff
    • Customer satisfaction: 4.8/5 rating on response quality and speed
    • Team efficiency: Sales reps focus 80% more on closing vs. admin tasks

    The solution was an AI-first CRM assistant that engages leads instantly, validates information, scores intent and guides sales toward closure.

    Problem and goals
    Leads were coming from multiple platforms with inconsistent data quality and manual follow-ups.
    Sales stages (inquiry, pricing, quotation, PI, closure) were not standardized or tracked intelligently.
    The company needed faster response time, better qualification and higher conversion with minimal manual effort.
    The objective: automate initial conversations, ensure data completeness and authenticity and give sales a “copilot” that recommends next-best-actions.

    Technical solution
    Channels and integration
    Integrated Twilio WhatsApp Business API to automatically initiate conversations as soon as a new lead is created in the CRM.
    Implemented an email integration layer to send auto-generated, personalized follow-up emails when WhatsApp engagement was low or when only an email was available.

    Agentic AI architecture
    Built a multi-agent system mapped to five key stages: Inquiry Received, Price Shared, Quotation Sharing, Proforma Invoice and Victory (closure).
    Used RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) so each agent could answer product questions (brand, model, configurations, purchase plans, consignee details) from internal knowledge bases while keeping responses accurate and contextual.
    Implemented agents for data capture and validation, product information verification and follow-up automation when users go inactive on WhatsApp or email.

    Intelligence and analytics
    Computed sentiment scores and “genuineness” signals to prioritize leads and adapt tone.
    Added negotiation-aware logic to recommend when to share prices, send quotations or push for proforma invoice.

    Chanakya analytics tab
    Aggregates customer sentiment and genuineness scores, likely intent/objections and recommended next steps so sales gets a decision-support layer from conversational data.

    Sarthi guidance tab
    Acts as a sales assistant that summarizes the current state of each lead, proposes the best next action and uses the agentic layer to simulate outcomes and choose a low-friction step forward.
